Episode 1: Sexual Healing

Welcome to Let Me Femsplain, a story-telling podcast hosted by Jasmine Rose-Olesco, where women talk and you listen. Sit back, relax and listen to Jasmine and Carly Lane discuss "Mad Max: Fury Road" and "Orphan Black". Jasmine also interviews Nellie Gayle about fandom culture, online communities, pop culture faves and the ways those topics relate to intersectional feminism.
